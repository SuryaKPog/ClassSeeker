ABOUT OUR PROJECT:
The ClassSeeker project is born out of our own experiences as students, grappling with the challenges of navigating crowded school environments. We understand firsthand the frustration of not being able to find a suitable place to sit and have lunch peacefully during hectic college hours. Often, we found ourselves wandering through crowded hallways, desperately searching for an empty classroom or a quiet corner, only to end up sitting on stairs or squeezing into cramped spaces. Recognizing the need for a solution to this common problem, we have developed ClassSeeker:a user-friendly platform designed specifically to address the needs of students like us. With ClassSeeker, students can easily locate available empty classrooms within their respective buildings, spanning multiple floors. By providing real-time information on vacant classrooms, the platform empowers students to efficiently find suitable spaces to enjoy their meals without the hassle of endless searching or uncomfortable alternatives. Our firsthand experience informs every aspect of ClassSeeker's intuitive interface and accurate data, ensuring that it enhances the overall campus experience and promotes a more relaxed and enjoyable environment for students during their break times.
